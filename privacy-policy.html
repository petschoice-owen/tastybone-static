Phishing

Phishing is a method used by fraudsters to deceive someone into giving sensitive information. Most of the time phishing will take the form of emails, which purport to be from well-known companies and ask recipients to send their private information. This information is then used for identity theft and fraud.

TastyBone will only ask you to send personal details by email if it is responding to a query you have sent it online and these details are needed to answer your query properly. TastyBone will never ask you to send any personal details by email unless you have contacted TastyBone first. If you have not contacted TastyBone please ignore any email claiming to be from TastyBone which asks for personal details, and let us know about it.
